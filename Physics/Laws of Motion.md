
## Law of Inertia
An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced externalforce.
$\sum\limits F=0 \Leftrightarrow v = constant$

## Law of Force
$F = ma = \dfrac{dp}{dt}$
where $p$ is momentum

## Law of Reaction
$F_{AB} = -F_{BA}$

- **Definition:** For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction. Whenever one object exerts a force on a second object, the second object exerts an equal and opposite force on the first.
- **Key Concept:** Forces always occur in pairs.
- **Example:** When a person walks, they push backward on the ground, and the ground pushes forward on the person with equal force. ![Britannica](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/faviconV2?url=https://www.britannica.com&client=AIM&size=128&type=FAVICON&fallback_opts=TYPE,SIZE,URL)Britannica +4
