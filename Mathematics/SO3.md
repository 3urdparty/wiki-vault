SO3 (Special Orthogonal group) is a [[Lie Group Theory|lie group]] (both a [[Group|group]] and a [[Manifolds|manifold]]) representing all possible [[3D Transformations|3D rotations]] (all possible $3×3$ rotation matrices).

It is a [[Manifolds|manifold]] because Rotations don't live in [[Euclidean Space]]; you can't add them directly to get a valid rotation matrix.  It is a [[Lie Group Theory|lie group]] because it satisfies the **4** criteria:
1. **Closure**: combining two rotations gives another rotation (closure),
2. rotation composition is **associative**
3. there's an **identity** rotation (do nothing)
4. every rotation has an **inverse** (rotate back)
